How to Book

All booking requests must be submitted in writing to ensure they are confirmed.

  • Step 1: Fill out the contact form.

  • Step 2: Choose your desired event date.

  • Step 3: Select your event type (private dining, corporate event, special occasion).

  • Step 4: Include your message.

  • Step 5: We will provide you with an estimated quote.

  • Step 6Submit your deposit to confirm the reservation.

Our Booking Policy

  • Please book your event as soon as possible to secure the date.

  • A deposit of 50% based on your estimated total bill is required to secure the date. This will be applied toward the final invoice total.

  • Final payment is due no later than 14 days before your event.

  • Final guest counts, menu selections, or changes are due no later than 14 days before the event.

Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancellations up to two weeks before your event: 50% of your required deposit will be refunded.

  • Cancellations received less than two weeks before your event will forfeit the entire required deposit.
